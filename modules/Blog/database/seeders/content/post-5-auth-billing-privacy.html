Three modules. Every SaaS needs all three. Saucebase ships them already built.

Auth module

The Auth module covers the full authentication surface:

  • Registration, login, and logout
  • Email verification and password reset
  • Social login via Laravel Socialite
  • Two-factor authentication
  • Role-based access with Spatie Laravel Permission (admin and user seeded by default)

Billing module

Stripe subscriptions, out of the box:

  • Plan management with trials, upgrades, and cancellations
  • User-facing billing portal
  • Webhooks pre-configured for payment failures and renewals
  • Stripe plan config lives in your config file — change plans, prices, and trials freely

Built on Laravel Cashier, fully copy-and-own.

Settings module

The account management pages your users expect:

  • Profile editing and avatar upload
  • Email change with re-verification
  • Password management
  • Notification preferences

Privacy and compliance

Cookie consent and GDPR scaffolding are handled at the app level — the kind of detail that's easy to overlook until it becomes a compliance problem.

Together, these modules cover the surface area that stops you from getting to what matters. They're not locked dependencies — they're starting points. Extend them, strip them down, or ship them exactly as-is. The choice is yours.
